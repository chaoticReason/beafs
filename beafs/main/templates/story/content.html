Full story of creation

Beafs.com is Anastasia Stankevich’s making, started as term paper on second year of bsuir.

The idea
My friend Arinochka was inflamed with term paper theme and suggest that she’ll participate. I agreed. Then we brainstormed and came up with a minimalistic player and a map application to find a company when going to some place. I felt in love with such smart map and she highly appreciated my player. We started to persuaded each other to take each ones’ projects. Finally I threw up a coin and that how we made our choice.

Process. Part 1
Doing all correct means start with a plan, with a block-scheme. By video calls to South Korea where Arinochka studied I discuss my thoughts and she offered her vision. By two month we had design of music player page. Unfortunately, Arina couldn’t fully participate because of midterm exams she had. I continued alone. So, now is a moment to say big thanks to her, I enjoyed the time.

Process. Part 2
In a month design was finished. After watching tutorials I wrote the back-end and front-end.
